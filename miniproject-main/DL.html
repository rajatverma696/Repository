How to Apply for a Driving License

Your step-by-step guide to obtaining a driving license easily.

Step-by-Step Driving License Application Process

Step 1: Check Eligibility

Ensure you meet the minimum age requirement and have the necessary documents for application.

Step 2: Gather Required Documents

  • Proof of Identity (e.g., Passport, Aadhaar Card)
  • Proof of Address (e.g., Utility Bill, Rental Agreement)
  • Passport-size Photographs
  • Medical Certificate (if required)

Step 3: Visit the Licensing Authority

Visit your local Regional Transport Office (RTO) or apply online through the official portal.

Step 4: Submit the Application Form

Fill out the required application form and submit it along with the supporting documents.

Step 5: Pay the Fees

Pay the applicable fees for the driving license and any additional services.

Step 6: Attend the Test

Appear for the driving test to demonstrate your knowledge and skills.

Step 7: Receive Your License

Upon passing the test, your driving license will be sent to your address or issued in person.

Additional Tips

  • Double-check all details on the application form to avoid errors.
  • Practice driving in a safe environment before the test.
  • Arrive early to the test center to avoid delays.
