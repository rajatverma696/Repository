Marriage Certificate Application Process

Follow these simple steps to apply for your marriage certificate in India.

Steps to Apply for a Marriage Certificate

Step 1: Register on the Official Portal

Visit the official state government website for marriage registration. Create an account by providing your details and generating a login ID.

Tip: Ensure you have a valid email ID and phone number.

Step 2: Fill Out the Application Form

Log in to the portal and locate the "Marriage Certificate Application" section. Provide details such as:

  • Name and age of both partners
  • Address details
  • Date and place of marriage

Note: Ensure all details match your official documents.

Step 3: Upload Supporting Documents

Submit scanned copies of the following:

  • Age proof (Birth certificate, Aadhaar card, etc.)
  • Address proof (Utility bills, Passport, etc.)
  • Marriage invitation card or marriage photo
  • Passport-sized photographs of both partners

Step 4: Book an Appointment

Select a convenient date and time to visit the Sub-Registrar's office for document verification.

Step 5: Visit the Sub-Registrar's Office

On the appointment date, visit the office with all original documents. Both partners and two witnesses must be present for verification.

Step 6: Receive the Certificate

After verification, the marriage certificate will be issued. You can download it from the portal or collect a hard copy from the office.

Important Tips

  • Ensure all details in the application form are accurate to avoid delays.
  • Carry original documents for verification along with photocopies.
  • Contact the local Sub-Registrar's office if you face any issues.
