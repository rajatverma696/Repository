How to Apply for a PAN Card

Your step-by-step guide to obtaining a Permanent Account Number (PAN) easily.

Step-by-Step PAN Card Application Process

Step 1: Visit the NSDL or UTIITSL Website

Access the PAN application form through the official NSDL website or UTIITSL website.

Step 2: Choose the Application Type

Select the appropriate form (Form 49A for Indian citizens or Form 49AA for foreign nationals).

Step 3: Fill in Your Details

Provide personal information like name, date of birth, and address. Ensure accuracy to avoid errors.

Step 4: Submit Supporting Documents

Upload scanned copies of identity proof, address proof, and a passport-size photo.

Step 5: Pay the Processing Fee

Pay the application fee online using net banking, credit/debit card, or UPI.

Step 6: Download the Acknowledgment

Once submitted, download and print the acknowledgment receipt for reference.

Step 7: Receive Your PAN Card

The PAN card will be sent to your registered address within 15-20 working days.
