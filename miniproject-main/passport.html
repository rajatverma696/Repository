Passport Application Process

Follow these simple steps to apply for your passport in India.

Steps to Apply for a Passport

Step 1: Check Eligibility

Ensure you meet the requirements for applying for a passport. Typically, this includes proof of nationality and residency.

Step 2: Gather Required Documents

Collect all necessary documents such as:

  • Proof of Identity (e.g., Aadhaar Card, Voter ID, Driving License)
  • Proof of Address (e.g., Utility Bill, Rental Agreement)
  • Birth Certificate or other proof of date of birth
  • Recent Passport-sized Photographs

Step 3: Register Online

Visit the official Passport Seva Portal, create an account, and fill in the application form.

Step 4: Pay Application Fee

Pay the applicable fee online using net banking, credit/debit card, or other payment options.

Step 5: Schedule an Appointment

Choose a convenient date and time for your visit to the Passport Seva Kendra (PSK) or Regional Passport Office (RPO).

Step 6: Visit the PSK/RPO

Attend your appointment, where you’ll submit documents, provide biometric data, and complete the verification process.

Step 7: Track Your Application

Use the tracking feature on the Passport Seva Portal to monitor your application’s progress.

Step 8: Receive Your Passport

After verification, your passport will be delivered to your registered address.

Important Tips

  • Ensure all documents are up-to-date and match the information in your application to avoid delays.
  • Contact the Passport Seva Kendra if you face any issues with your application.
