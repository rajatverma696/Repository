How to Apply for an Aadhaar Card

Your step-by-step guide to obtaining your Aadhaar card easily.

Step-by-Step Aadhaar Application Process

Step 1: Locate an Aadhaar Enrollment Center

Visit the nearest Aadhaar enrollment center. You can find a center using the official UIDAI website.

Step 2: Fill the Application Form

Collect and fill out the Aadhaar application form available at the center or download it from the UIDAI website.

Step 3: Submit Supporting Documents

Provide proof of identity (POI) and proof of address (POA) documents. Accepted documents include your passport, voter ID, driving license, and utility bills.

Step 4: Provide Biometric Data

At the center, your photograph, fingerprints, and iris scan will be captured for Aadhaar registration.

Step 5: Receive Acknowledgment Slip

After completing the process, you will receive an acknowledgment slip containing your enrollment ID for tracking.

Step 6: Check Status and Download Aadhaar

Use your enrollment ID to check the status of your Aadhaar application on the UIDAI website. Once generated, you can download the e-Aadhaar.

Why Do You Need an Aadhaar Card?

  • Serves as a proof of identity and address.
  • Mandatory for availing government subsidies and benefits.
  • Required for filing income tax returns.
  • Link it with your bank account for seamless financial transactions.
