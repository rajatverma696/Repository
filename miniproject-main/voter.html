How to Apply for a Voter ID

Your step-by-step guide to obtaining a Voter ID easily.

Step-by-Step Voter ID Application Process

Step 1: Visit the Official Website

Go to the official Election Commission website or visit your local Election office to begin your application process.

Step 2: Fill the Application Form

Download and fill out Form 6 for new voter registration. Alternatively, you can fill it out online.

Step 3: Submit Documents

You will need to provide proof of identity (Aadhaar, Passport, or other valid documents) and proof of residence (utility bills, lease agreement, etc.).

Step 4: Attend the Voter Registration Center

Submit your form and documents at your nearest Voter Registration Center. Your photo and biometric data will be captured.

Step 5: Receive Acknowledgment

After your documents are processed, you will receive an acknowledgment slip, which will contain your reference number for tracking your application.

Step 6: Receive Voter ID

Your Voter ID will be sent to your registered address. You can also download an e-Voter ID from the official website after approval.

Why Apply for Voter ID?

  • Required for voting in elections across India.
  • Acts as a valid government-issued photo ID for various services.
  • Helps in establishing your citizenship and identity.

Helpful Tips for a Smooth Application

  • Ensure all details in the form are accurate to avoid delays.
  • Check the required documents before submitting your application.
  • Track your application status regularly using the reference number.
